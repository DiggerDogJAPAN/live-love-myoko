About this accommodation

Blending modern comfort with traditional Japanese charm, this beautifully renovated home offers a warm and inviting base for your stay in Myoko Kogen. Thoughtfully redesigned throughout, the house retains its authentic Japanese character with traditional finishes, carefully selected artwork, and design details that reflect the area's heritage.

The property comfortably accommodates up to 8 guests across five bedrooms, making it ideal for families, groups of friends, or longer stays. A spacious living room on the main floor provides a welcoming place to relax after a day on the slopes or exploring the region, while a second lounge upstairs offers additional space to unwind, read, or enjoy time with family and friends.

The newly renovated kitchen and dining area are fully equipped for preparing meals and gathering together, combining modern convenience with a stylish Japanese aesthetic. Two toilets are located on the ground floor for added comfort and practicality.

For winter guests, the house features a dedicated dry room for storing and drying ski and snowboard equipment, as well as a covered parking space for one vehicle. Every room is heated with modern air conditioning units, ensuring a comfortable stay throughout the year.

Whether you're visiting for the ski season, the vibrant green season, or simply to experience life in the Japanese Alps, this thoughtfully restored home offers the perfect balance of tradition, comfort, and convenience.
