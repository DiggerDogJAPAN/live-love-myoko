About this accommodation

Everything you need to enjoy the best of Myoko!

Located in the quieter area of Myoko Kogen, away from the noise and crowds of Akakura Onsen, Mountain Base Myoko is the perfect retreat for families and groups seeking peace, comfort, and the charm of a traditional Japanese neighborhood — while still being just minutes from resorts, restaurants, and everyday conveniences.

Mountain Base Myoko blends modern comfort with the warmth of a lived-in home, offering a peaceful retreat in a traditional neighborhood at the foot of Mt. Myoko. Enjoy spacious rooms, mountain views, air conditioning throughout, central heating, and cozy down duvets that make winter stays especially inviting. The large modern kitchen is fully equipped for group meals, while fast Wi-Fi, Netflix, books, and games make it easy to relax after a day on the slopes. Designed for families and groups, the lodge comfortably seats 10 around the dining table and provides the perfect balance of quiet village life and easy access to resorts, restaurants, and local amenities.

  • 4 Double Bedrooms, 1 Single & 1 Japanese style tatami room that can fit up to 4.
  • Air conditioning in all rooms.
  • Smart TV with Netflix etc (requires your account)
  • Parking for 3 cars.
  • Microwave, toaster, coffee machine, rice cooker, dishwasher & kettle in fully equipped kitchen.
  • Two large modern rain style showers.
  • Basic supplies & local coffee supplied.
  • Central heating in hallways.
  • Large dry room for gear.
  • Cleaning during your stay.
  • Fast WiFi.
  • Books and games supplied.
  • Vast local knowledge to help you enjoy your stay.
