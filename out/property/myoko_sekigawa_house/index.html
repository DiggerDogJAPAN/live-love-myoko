Overview

This large residential property sits on freehold land in a convenient central location in Myoko village. With easy road access and available car parking spaces, the property offers both practicality and potential for a variety of uses.

The house provides generous interior space, including 3–4 bedroom areas, one bathroom, and two toilets. The layout also features a spacious kitchen, dining, and living area, making it well suited for comfortable family living or hosting guests.

The property includes a snowblower, which is particularly useful during Myoko’s winter months. The house will be emptied after the sale, though the buyer is welcome to keep any items they find useful.

With its size and layout, the property could make an ideal holiday home or could potentially be converted into a guest house or accommodation for visitors to the Myoko area.

The house is connected to a sewage tank system that requires cleaning during the year. The roof may also benefit from painting in the future, which should be considered as part of normal property maintenance.

Overall, this property offers a great opportunity to purchase a well-located home in central Myoko with plenty of space and potential for personal or hospitality use.
