Overview

This prime parcel of land is located in the heart of Akakura Onsen, one of Japan’s most recognised resort areas. The site enjoys an excellent position with direct road access and close proximity to the ski fields, accommodation, restaurants, and hot spring facilities that make Akakura a year-round destination.

The land has a total area of 474 m² and is well suited for development or long-term investment. With utilities such as water, sewage, and electricity available nearby, the property offers a practical and attractive foundation for future use.

Akakura Onsen remains one of the most sought-after locations in Myoko due to its combination of:

  • winter tourism demand
  • Limited availability of land
  • Ongoing interest from domestic and overseas buyers

This site is ideal for those looking to:

  • Secure land in a premium resort location
  • Develop a future holiday home or lodge
  • Hold as a strategic investment in a tightly held market


Opportunities to purchase land in Akakura Onsen are increasingly rare, making this a compelling option for buyers seeking location, accessibility, and long-term value.
