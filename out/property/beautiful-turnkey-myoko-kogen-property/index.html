Overview

Located in the heart of Myoko, this spacious mountain home offers an unbeatable central location, with easy access to ski resorts, restaurants, and local amenities. Whether you're looking for a ready-to-operate minpaku (short-term rental) or a comfortable family holiday home, this property is perfectly suited for both.

The house features six generously sized bedrooms, many of which enjoy stunning views of Mt. Myoko, creating a true alpine retreat experience. Designed for practicality in heavy snow conditions, the home benefits from a self-shedding roof, significantly reducing the need for snow clearing during winter.

Additional features include covered garage parking for 3–4 vehicles, making it ideal for guests or large families. The property also comes equipped with a snowblower and security camera system, allowing for easy maintenance and peace of mind. The home is situated on leased land, with two years of land rent already prepaid, offering immediate convenience for the new owner.
