Overview

This impressive 6SLDK detached home is located in the Minamitakada area of Joetsu City, Niigata, offering both space and functionality in a quiet residential setting.

Constructed in July 2009, the property uses FP construction methods, known for high insulation and energy efficiency. The home is fully electric, providing a modern and cost-efficient living environment.

The building spans approximately 297.09㎡ across three floors, sitting on a large 431.47㎡ plot of residential land. The layout includes a spacious 30-tatami LDK, multiple bedrooms, and additional storage rooms (S), making it highly suitable for large families, guest accommodation, or shared living arrangements.

The structure is a combination of wood and reinforced concrete with a galvanized steel roof, ensuring durability.
