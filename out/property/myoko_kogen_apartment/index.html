Overview

Located in the heart of Myoko Kogen, Etore Akakura offers a lifestyle that is difficult to find elsewhere in the region. Designed for owners who want to enjoy mountain living without the responsibilities and maintenance of a standalone house, the complex provides a wide range of facilities and professional management, making ownership simple and stress-free.

Residents have access to an on-site onsen, indoor swimming pool, ski room, laundry facilities, games area, spacious lobby, car parking, and tennis courts. Whether visiting for a weekend getaway or an extended stay, everything you need is conveniently available within the complex.

One of the biggest advantages of ownership at Etore Akakura is the ease of maintenance. With common areas professionally managed and building maintenance taken care of, owners can enjoy the benefits of living in Myoko without worrying about snow clearing, exterior maintenance, or ongoing property upkeep. It is truly one of the easiest and most worry-free ways to own property in the area.

The monthly maintenance fee is approximately ¥30,000 - ¥40,000, covering the ongoing management and operation of the complex and its facilities.

For buyers seeking a secure, low-maintenance property with excellent amenities and year-round enjoyment, Etore Akakura represents outstanding value and a rare opportunity in one of Myoko's most established apartment developments.
