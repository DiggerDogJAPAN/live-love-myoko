Arai - Great potential property

¥ 5,000,000

This spacious property in Hakusan-cho, Myoko City sits on a generous 611 m² land parcel and features a two-storey, 6SDK wooden home built in 1978, offering a total floor area of 171 m². The house includes multiple living spaces, two toilets, and parking for one vehicle, making it well-suited for family use or renovation into a holiday home. An additional two-storey garage and storage building provides extra utility. The property benefits from public water, city gas, electricity, and a septic system, and is conveniently positioned on corner access with roads on both the north and west sides. Located in a quiet low-rise residential zone, this property is offered at an attractive price of ¥5 million.
