Overview

This spacious detached home is located in Kurohime, just 15 minutes from Myoko Kogen, offering a great balance of countryside living with convenient access to transport and amenities.

The property sits on a generous 356.78 m² residential land plot and is a two-storey wooden house with a total floor area of approximately 111 m² (94.77 m² on the ground floor and 16.65 m² on the second floor).

Inside, the house offers five bedrooms, making it well suited for family living, staff accommodation, or use as a holiday home. There is a private driveway with on-site parking, providing easy access year-round.

The location is great:

  • Close to Kurohime
  • Convenient access to Myoko Kogen
  • Near Kurohime Station
  • Easy highway access via Shinanomachi

With strong transport links, ample space, and a practical layout, this home is ideal for those seeking a comfortable base near the Myoko area while remaining well connected to the wider region.
